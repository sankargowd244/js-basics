Client Solutions Overview



Key enterprise Benifits




 Move the Metric

Businesses created awareness and engagement around operational metrics, critical to their service model on Myfriday. Enterprises have found that the mean score of the metric can be moved by as much as 25% through this approach.

  Reduce the Range

By working on the outliers and the average into a narrower, manageable band, organizations can plan their next wave of metric improvement in a collaborative manner.

  Increase Manager Bandwidth

Myfriday’s AIR framework automates insight delivery, both to managers and first line employees. This minimizes the time spent on data crunching. Both managers and first line employees are on the same page, allowing them to focus on coaching and guidance when they meet.

  Consistancy in communication

Myfriday’s collaborative engagement toolkit allows for creation and cross pollination of insights and actions across the organization. This predictable approach improves employee morale and participation in organizational performance improvement initiatives.

  Rewards and Incentives

Myfriday ensures objectivity in reward distribution by aligning the rewards to improvement in metrics through predefined business rules. This approach is well received by front line employees and managers as it allows them to focus on business outcomes with clarity.

  Measure effective of Initiatives

Myfriday enables enterprises to go beyond performance campaigns to measure their effectiveness and fine tune their future approach.
